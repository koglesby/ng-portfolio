Throughout the course of the camp, I built my first web applications with Ruby on Rails, learned computer science fundamentals, and collaborated with other students to build our capstone project. Since completing the camp, I have continued to widen my coding knowledge-base and improve my skills and practices, to be best equipped solve problems, write clean effective code, and build awesome applications.
